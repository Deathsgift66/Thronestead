Kingmaker's Rise — Tutorial

Step 1: Establish Your Kingdom

Create your kingdom name and ruler identity. This is your legacy in the world of Kingmaker’s Rise!

Step 2: Manage Resources

Gather food, wood, stone, gold, and other critical resources to sustain your kingdom and armies.

Step 3: Construct Buildings

Develop villages and cities with military, economic, and cultural buildings to strengthen your realm.

Step 4: Train Troops

Recruit soldiers, knights, and siege weapons. Build an army capable of defending your lands and conquering rivals.

Step 5: Diplomacy & Alliances

Forge alliances, sign treaties, and participate in the Treaty Web. Diplomacy can win wars before swords are drawn.

Step 6: Declare War

Master combat tactics, understand troop types and counters, and lead your armies to victory in strategic wars.

Step 7: Expand Influence

Influence the world stage with temples, laws, and world events. Shape the history of Kingmaker’s Rise.

Step 8: Participate in the Market

Engage in the kingdom economy by buying and selling goods on the open market — manage trade, profit, and logistics.

Step 9: Monitor Notifications

Stay informed with the Notifications system — track alliance updates, war events, diplomacy, and resource status.

Step 10: Master Your Kingdom

Combine all systems: economy, military, diplomacy, law, faith, and war to become a true Kingmaker. Your legend begins!
